Case 1 Objective: Increase In-Store Sales Through Pricing Optimization 6
Positive Insight:
In-store sales grew by 66.5% YoY, contributing a total of $33.5 million in revenue. This reflects strong customer demand for in-store products and signals the potential to further capitalize on this growth.



Negative Insight:
Discounts for in-store purchases increased by 62.8% YoY, which negatively impacts profitability.
The average order quantity for in-store sales is 5 items, suggesting there is room to improve sales per transaction through upselling and bundling.


Case 1 ROI:
By optimizing the discount strategy (reducing discounts by 10%) and introducing upselling techniques, a 5% revenue increase can be achieved. This could result in an additional $1.675 million in sales for the in-store channel.
