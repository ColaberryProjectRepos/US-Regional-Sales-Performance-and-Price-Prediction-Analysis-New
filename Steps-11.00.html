Importing Data 11

US Regional Sales Dataset 


1.Download the US_Regional_Sales file by clicking the red icon above

2.    First you need to have the data save on your device.  

3.    Open Power BI Desktop.

4.    Click on Get data ribbon

5.    Select Excel Workbook and then click connect

6.    Choose “US_Regional_Sales ” and  click open

7.    Select the table, a preview of data will be displayed, click on Load.
