Visualizations
The Power BI dashboard visualizes key metrics from the US Regional Sales data, focusing on Total Revenue, Average Unit Price, Average Order Quantity, and Sales Channel performance across regions and years. It provides a comprehensive view of year-over-year growth in sales, highlighting the impact of discounts and unit prices on revenue. The dashboard also offers insights into regional performance, product category trends, and the effectiveness of each sales channel (In-Store, Online, Distributor, Wholesale).
Key visualizations track how factors such as discount application and order quantity correlate with sales performance, supporting data-driven decisions for optimizing pricing strategies, inventory management, and sales channel allocation to maximize profitability.
