Creating Calendar Table 13



1.    Go to Modeling Tab click on new table.

2.    Type the following DAX formula on DAX formula bar, click on the check mark and save.



Calendar Year =

ADDCOLUMNS(

    FILTER(

        CALENDARAUTO(),

        YEAR([Date]) >= 2018 && YEAR([Date]) <= 2020  

    ),

    "Year", YEAR([Date]),

    "Month Number", MONTH([Date]),

    "Month Name Short", FORMAT([Date], "MMM"),

    "Quarter", "Q" & QUARTER([Date]),

    "Year Quarter", FORMAT([Date], "YYYY") & " Q" & QUARTER([Date]),

    "Week Number", WEEKNUM([Date], 2),

    "Day of Week", WEEKDAY([Date], 2),

    "Weekday Name", FORMAT([Date], "dddd")

)
