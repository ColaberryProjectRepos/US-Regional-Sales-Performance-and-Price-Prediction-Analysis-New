Lessons Learned
Data Quality: We encountered initial challenges with data redundancy, inconsistencies, and incompleteness. These were overcome through a rigorous cleaning and validation process.
Data Acquisition: The initial dataset was insufficient for a comprehensive understanding. We addressed this by acquiring supplementary data from various sources.
Model Optimization: Certain DAX formulas needed refinement to guarantee accurate results. We achieved this through iterative testing and adjustments.
Visualization Clarity: Effective communication of insights hinged on appropriate visualizations. Refining these visuals ensured clear understanding of the data.


Future Capabilities
Enhanced Classification Models:
Exploring more advanced machine learning models, such as ensemble methods and neural networks, can improve classification accuracy and help predict sales channels more precisely.
Real-Time Data Integration:
Integrating live sales data and customer feedback will enable more timely and dynamic sales predictions, allowing the business to react to market changes in real-time.
Vendor and Sales Channel Collaboration:
Strengthening partnerships with key vendors and sales channels can lead to improved demand forecasting and better inventory management strategies, optimizing sales predictions.
Automation of Predictive Analytics:
Automating the process of data cleaning, feature engineering, and model retraining will streamline operations and allow more focus on strategic decision-making based on accurate, real-time insights.
