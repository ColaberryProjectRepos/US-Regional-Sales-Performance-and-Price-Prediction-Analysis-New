Methodology 4

Data Preparation
Data was cleaned by identifying and removing duplicates, handling missing values, filtering outliers, and standardizing the data to ensure consistency and reliability, supporting the analysis of sales performance and profitability.


Model Selection
The KNeighborsClassifier model was selected due to its strong predictive performance, achieving an accuracy of 96.0%. It outperformed other models in precision (96.3%) and recall (96.0%), making it highly reliable for predicting sales channels based on key order features. The model’s high F1 score (95.9%) further confirms its balanced performance in both precision and recall, ensuring accurate predictions for all sales channels.


Model Development
The KNeighborsClassifier model was trained and fine-tuned using historical sales and order data from multiple sales channels. The model's parameters were optimized to improve its ability to predict the most effective sales channel for each order while minimizing classification errors. Key features, such as Order Quantity, Unit Price, and Product Information, were selected to ensure that the model captures the relationships among relevant variables. The development process involved multiple iterations to optimize the model's performance, making it a reliable tool for predicting sales channels and improving resource allocation strategies.
